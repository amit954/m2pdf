The standard "Print to PDF" in Gmail cuts off text and completely ignores your attachments. Discover the professional way to export emails cleanly, directly from your inbox.

The Problem with Gmail's Default Print Feature

If you've ever tried hitting `Ctrl+P` or using the built-in "Print all" icon in Gmail, you already know the frustration. The default system creates messy documents, includes unnecessary sidebar menus, and worst of all, it completely leaves behind your important attachments.

The M2pdf Solution

M2pdf is built directly into the native Gmail UI. You don't need to forward your sensitive emails to a third-party service. By running locally in your browser, it captures the exact formatting of the email thread and bundles the original attachments into a clean, organized folder structure.

Key Advantages

  • Zero-Knowledge Privacy: Processing happens locally.
  • Native UI: Operates right inside Gmail.
  • Attachment Preservation: Files are saved automatically.
  • Gmail Search: Export emails using Gmail search and filters.
  • Clean Layouts: Removes unwanted headers and links.
