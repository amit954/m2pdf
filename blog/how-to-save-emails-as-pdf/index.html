How to Save Emails as PDF - Quick and Easy Methods

February 14, 2025
Photo by Stephen Phillips on Unsplash

We receive countless emails every day, and as our inboxes are drowning in emails, saving important emails as PDF has become an essential practice for businesses, professionals, and individuals to archive important business communications, maintain legal records, or simply organize personal communications. Converting your emails to pdf is great for preserving the exact content, formatting, and visual elements of your messages in a universally compatible format.

This guide explores the best ways to save your emails as PDF documents.

Easy Methods to Convert Emails to PDF

Built-in solution

Most popular email services including Gmail, Yahoo and Outlook Web provide dedicated print button to print emails. Here are the steps to save emails as pdf using the built-in method.

  1. Open the email
  2. Look for the printer icon in your email view or find "Print" in the email options menu (usually three dots or More options)
  3. Once in the print preview, select "Save as PDF" from the destination dropdown in the print options
  4. Choose your save location and save the pdf file
